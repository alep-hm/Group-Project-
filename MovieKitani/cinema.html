CINEMAS

Times Cineplex

2012 - current

Established in 2012, the first location was opened at Times Square Shopping Center. Years later, Times Cineplex expanded its circuit at Empire Hotel and Country Club that features VIP seats and contemporary design. In the same year, another location was introduced at Tutong. In 2015, the latest branch was opened at KB Sentral. Times Cineplex operates a total of 16 screens in these 4 locations across Brunei Darussalam.
